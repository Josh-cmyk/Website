Bike repair, anywhere

Flat tire on the way to work? Bike just needs some TLC? Don't want to stress about getting to a bike shop, paying for the inconvenience or having to wait a few days for the repair? Simply input your location and the desired time of repair, we will ensure your bike will be ready to go within 45 minutes. 

We provide quick mobile repairs within the AB Travel Zone (within the ring bahn) in Berlin. 




Founder Gavriel Vinegrad moved to Berlin in 2019 with a desire to utilise his passion for cycling and fixing bicycles to provide an innovative bike repair service to meet Berlin's increasing bicycle usage. 


CLEAR PRICING

We provide cost breakdown of repairs online so you know in advance how much a repair will cost you.                                                                        

FAST

Get back on the road fast. We will call you on arrival at your scheduled appointment time. Repair will take less than 45 minutes. Contrast this on an average repair time of 3 days at a static bike shop.

 

FAIR

Improving lives is a priority for us. We provide a professional and friendly mobile bicycle repairs for a similar price to static bike shops (excluding travel cost).


CONVENIENT

No need to stress about whether there is a local bike shop that will be open at times that fit with your work schedule. 



We provide small bicycle repairs and servicing at the customers location 

We currently can not support repair of: disc brakes, bikes with internal gears/brakes, xxx. If you need these, we advise you to take your bike to a repair workshop. 

Please use the form provided to place your order.

 In "Your Order Details" please include the following information:  

  1. Your full address, including name on bell
  2. Desired time (or availability) of appointment 
  3. Repairs needed
  4. Size of your wheels- this is normally written on the side of your tires and is usually in the format 
    1. 28×1.25  
    2. 26×2.0 
    3. 700x25c


 

﻿
We fix or replace wheels/tires, gearshift and drive, brakes, lights, handlebars and saddles. We also conduct general servicing and cleaning.

The price for a home visit is €45, which includes changing a cable, brake blocks or changing an inner-tube. In addition, we also include a full service including: brakes and gear adjustment, pumping up tires and oiling your chain.

Gavriel started his facebook page and mobile bicycle business in 2019 in order to pursue his twin passions of urban cycling and fixing bikes. It soon became clear that expanding his one man business would be necessary to meet the increasing demand for home bicycle repair in Berlin. As such, fahrradfixi.de was created as a digital platform to efficiently provide centralised customer information and streamline the ordering process.
