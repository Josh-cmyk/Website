Fast bike repair, wherever you need it

Flat tire on the way to work? Bike just needs some TLC? Don't want to stress about getting to a bike shop, paying for the inconvenience or having to wait a few days for the repair? Simply input your location and the desired time of repair, and our mechanics will ensure your bike will be ready to go within 45 minutes. 

We provide quick mobile repairs within the AB Travel Zone (within the ring bahn) in Berlin. 


"After living in the Netherlands for 5 years, cycling has become an integral part of my lifestyle. It has the benefits of being fast, low cost, healthy and environmentally friendly." - Josh, founder



CLEAR COSTS

We provide cost breakdown of repairs online in advance so you know in advance how much a repair will cost you. It is also possible to pay for the service by paypal.                                                                         

FAST

Get back on the road fast. Ordering takes 5 minutes and our mechanic will be with you within 30 minutes or at your scheduled appointment, and will call you on arrival. Repair will take less than 45 minutes. Contrast this on an average repair time of 3 days at a static bike shop. 

FAIR

Improving lives is a priority for us. We provide a professional and friendly mobile bicycle repairs for a similar price to static bike shops (excluding travel cost). Also, all our mechanics are paid a living wage, with performance based bonuses.


CONVENIENT

No need to stress about whether there is a local bike shop that will be open at times that fit with your work schedule. Minor repairs can also be completed while you work, in which case unlocking the bike and meeting the mechanic would not even be necessary. 

In 2010, 500,000 people commuted by bicycle daily composing 13.1% of total journeys. There are and estimated 71 bicycles per 100 residents in Berlin, and over 1000km of cycle paths. 

A fast, professional location based bike repair and servicing in Berlin. We aim to facilitate all forms of cycling as a primary form of transport in Berlin. In future, we aim to extend to "instant" repairs, where a local mechanic will be flagged instantly and arrive within 10 minutes meaning the bike will be mobile within 30-60 minutes of the breakdown.
